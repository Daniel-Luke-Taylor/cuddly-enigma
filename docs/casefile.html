Identifying Marks: Officers haven’t been close enough to properly identify but is believed to have been seen fleeing crime scene with a limp after landing incorrectly during a chase on foot

Description: Identifying characteristics have not been able to be seen or described by officers, as the killer has been seen fleeing past crime scenes wearing dark clothing that obscures all aspects of appearance.

Modus Operandi: Killer has been able to avoid DNA analysis, as crime scenes have been methodically cleaned by the time officers have arrived. No traces of DNA could be found on any of the past victim’s corpses, with all weapons and instruments removed from the scene, assumed to be with the killer.

The killer seems to be very methodical and almost ritualistic in the killing of his victims, with graphic crime scenes with the victims displayed similar to a tableau left behind for officers to discover.

The killer seems to wish for everyone to know of his crimes, as victims are displayed in public places in a similar fashion as artwork would be displayed.
Charges: Several counts of murder
